DT Article
ID VAPOR-PHASE EPITAXY
AB High quality Ga0.47In0.53As/InP quantum well structures have been grown on InP substrates by OMVPE.  4.2 K photoluminescence (PL) linewidths of single quantum wells (SQW's) were the narrowest ever reported by OMVPE, and were comparable to those of the best SQW's grown by chemical beam epitaxy.  A multiple quantum well (MQW) has been grown with precisely controlled periodicity as well as excellent uniformity over a 2-inch diameter wafer.  Excitonic peaks in absorption spectra of the MQW were observed at room temperature.
